2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on a device by a website or application to store information about user preferences or sessions. Similar technologies include web beacons, pixel tags, local storage objects, and device fingerprinting — all of which can be used to recognize users and track behavior across sessions or platforms.

These technologies are widely used to make applications work, to improve efficiency, to provide analytics, or to deliver targeted advertising.

3. Our Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

🍪 Elephant Bet – Sports Events does not use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, local analytics storage, advertising identifiers, or any similar tracking technologies to collect or store information about you.

Specifically, our Application does not use:

  • Session cookies – temporary cookies used to remember your actions during a session
  • Persistent cookies – cookies that remain on your device after a session ends
  • Analytics cookies – cookies used to collect usage statistics (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising cookies – cookies used to serve targeted ads or measure ad performance
  • Functional cookies – cookies used to remember your preferences or personalization
  • Third-party cookies – cookies placed by external parties within the Application
  • Device fingerprinting – collection of device attributes to create a unique identifier
  • Advertising identifiers – mobile ad IDs such as GAID (Google Advertising ID) or IDFA

4. Why We Don't Use Cookies

Our Application is designed to function without any need for tracking or data collection. We do not run advertising within the Application, we do not analyze user behavior, and we do not personalize content based on user history. As a result, there is no technical or business need to deploy cookies or similar technologies.

This approach reflects our commitment to user privacy and simplicity.
